DO MORE

Snorkeling Adventures

Explore the vibrant underwater world of Taniti! Dive into the crystal-clear waters off the island’s coast and discover colorful coral reefs, schools of tropical fish, and sea turtles. Whether you're a beginner or experienced, Taniti offers guided snorkeling tours for all levels.

Rainforest Zip-Line Experience

Get your adrenaline pumping as you zip-line through Taniti’s lush tropical rainforests. Enjoy breathtaking views of the island’s greenery and unique wildlife as you soar above the treetops. Perfect for thrill-seekers looking for an unforgettable adventure!

Helicopter Rides Over the Volcano

Take a thrilling helicopter ride to Taniti’s active volcano. Witness the awe-inspiring sight of the volcanic crater and its surroundings from the sky. This unique aerial experience provides a once-in-a-lifetime view of the island’s rugged landscape.

Nightlife at Merriton Landing

Experience the vibrant nightlife of Taniti at Merriton Landing! Dance the night away at the island’s newest dance club, enjoy a pint at the local microbrewery, or relax at one of the several lively pubs. A fun-filled evening is guaranteed!
