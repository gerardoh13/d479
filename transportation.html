Public Transportation in Taniti City

Getting around Taniti City is easy and affordable with public buses that run from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. daily. For a scenic way to explore, rental bikes are also available. Visitors can enjoy the flat, walkable streets of the city and surrounding areas.

Explore Merriton Landing by Foot

Merriton Landing is the perfect area to explore on foot. From restaurants to entertainment venues, everything is within walking distance. Enjoy the charming atmosphere of this rapidly developing area as you stroll through its scenic streets.
